# wg_WGMLEARN
Working group on machine learning in marine science (WGMLEARN)

The field of machine learning and AI is developing rapidly, and these technologies are and will be instrumental for marine research and resource management. As the technology is explored and established, other ICES expert groups are seeking advice, comments, or other inputs, which WGMLEARN aims to provide. It is also important to identify challenges unique to the marine domain that current mainstream ML research fails to adequately address. Highlighting these challenges and linking to appropriate training sets and test cases will help steer ML research towards high-value opportunities in marine science and management.
