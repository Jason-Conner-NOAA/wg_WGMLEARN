#Outreach

##Description
Establish contact with other ICES working groups to explore collaboration opportunities and to chart research and knowledge needs. Support the development of aspects of AI at the ICES strategic level.

##Background
To prioritize research and dissemination effort, it is necessary to develop and maintain good communications with the users of ML technology.

##Deliverables
List of networking contacts (e.g., in the form of a mailing list or similar), participation in relevant meetings, symposia, or similar.
