#Identify unmet challenges

##Description
Develop a list of relevant topics and tasks for the application of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML), with emphasis on unmet challenges specific to the marine domain and potential benefits. Integrate the output from WKAAII and link to relevant ICES working groups, research groups, and public datasets.

##Background
There is a need to identify gaps in the application of ML in marine sciences to focus future efforts.

##Deliverables
Review paper (food-for-thought format).
