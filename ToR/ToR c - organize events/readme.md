#Organize and execute

##Description
Based on the outcomes of ToR a and b, organize a series of 5–10 events to target important challenges. These can take the form of tutorials, hackathons, workshops, or conference sessions, co-organized with relevant ICES working groups or other entities. Each event will lead to a written report.

##Background
This ToR aims to build capacity within the marine science community on the use of ML and leverage the skills and knowledge of the community. The aim is to bring people together to foster collaboration and innovation.

##Deliverables
Tutorials:
Video recordings and reusable learning modules are made available online for community use.

Hackathons:
Open-source code repositories (e.g., GitHub) containing prototype algorithms, workflows, or data products, and preparation of scientific or technical peer-reviewed manuscripts.
